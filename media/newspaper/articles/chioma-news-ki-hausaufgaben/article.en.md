## Lukas and his homework

Lukas had math homework. Instead of thinking it through, he asked ChatGPT – and copied the answer. "I just got some help," he said. But did he really?

## What's the difference?

Using AI to understand how something works – that's learning. Copying the finished answer – that's something else. Your brain didn't learn anything in the process. Next time, you'll know just as little as before.

## When does AI actually make you better?

When you use it as a training partner: ask for the path, not the solution. Have it explain why something works the way it does. Then check for yourself whether you understood it.

That's how you grow – instead of just getting your homework done.

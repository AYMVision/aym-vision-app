# 🎙️ Your own audio piece

Hey, Carlos here from the tech section.

Want to make your own audio piece?  
It's easier than you might think.

The apps I mention are ones we've used ourselves.  
But there are plenty of others that work just as well.

👉 For some you'll need an account.  
Best to record your first audio piece together with an adult.

Let's go.

---

[[img src="media/newspaper/articles/tip-carlos-audio-howto/aufnahme-setup" alt="Child recording audio with a smartphone in a quiet environment."]]

## 🧠 1. Idea & plan

Think briefly:

- What is it about?
- What do you want to say?
- What could the flow look like?

👉 You don't need a perfect plan.  
A rough outline is plenty.

---

## 🎧 2. Start recording

A smartphone or tablet is enough.

Got headphones with a microphone?  
Perfect.

[[callout kind="info" title="Important"]]
- Quiet place  
- No background music  
- Close the window  
[[/callout]]

Then simply:

- Open voice memo app  
- Press "Record"  
- Start talking

Stumbled over a word? Doesn't matter. 😉  
Pros edit later too.

---

## ✂️ 3. Listen back & edit

Listen to your recording.

- Start a bit shaky?  
- Too long?

Then you can edit.

For example with:
- GarageBand (iPhone)
- Audacity (PC)

👉 Goal: short, clear, compelling

---

[[img src="media/newspaper/articles/tip-carlos-audio-howto/schneiden-audio" alt="Audio being edited on a laptop or tablet."]]

## 🗳️ 4. Opinion check

Make your piece interactive.

Think of a question, for example:  
"Are you for or against it?"

And include:
- 3 answer options

This works with, for example:
- Google Forms  
- Mentimeter  
- your school platform

👉 That way others can think along and have a say.

---

## 📤 5. Upload & share

Now it gets serious:

- Upload audio  
- Link to poll  
- Done 🎉

[[callout kind="warn" title="Watch out"]]
Think carefully about who is allowed to see or hear your piece.  
Only share it with people you trust.
[[/callout]]

[[/checklist]]

## 💡 My conclusion

You need:

- no expensive equipment  
- no studio  
- no professional  

👉 Just an idea.  

👉 A device.  

👉 And a little courage.

Give it a try.

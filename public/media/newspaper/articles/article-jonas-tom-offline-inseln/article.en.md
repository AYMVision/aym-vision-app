# Always online? Tom shows there's another way 📵

I'm sitting here with Tom after school.

And really:  
He doesn't have his phone in his hand.

Just his bike standing next to him.

Now I'm curious.  
I want to ask him about his offline islands.


[[img src="media/newspaper/articles/article-jonas-tom-offline-inseln/jonas-interviewt-tom" alt="Jonas interviews Tom after school next to his bike."]]

## 📵 Tom's Offline Model

**Jonas:** You're rarely online.  
How do you do it?

**Tom:** Not hard.  
I follow my own rules.

**Jonas:** Your own rules?

**Tom:** Yes.

**Jonas:** Otherwise you lose control at some point?

**Tom:** Could be.  
Never tried not to.

**Jonas:** Seems to work.  
What rules?

---

## 🔕 Notifications off

**Tom:** Most important thing first:  
I've turned off all notifications.

**Jonas:** All of them? 😳

**Tom:** Yes.  
You don't need them.

If the phone keeps buzzing, you're no longer the one deciding.  
The phone decides.

---

## ⏰ Two fixed times

**Tom:** I only go online at certain times.

**Jonas:** When?

**Tom:** Before school.  
And briefly again after 5 pm.

If I have plans, I make an exception.

Not in between.

**Jonas:** Isn't that hard?

**Tom:** Maybe at first.  
Then it becomes normal.

---

## 🌳 Offline islands

**Tom:** I have times when my phone is simply gone.

**Jonas:** Completely gone?

**Tom:** Yes.  
When riding.  
When skating.  
When I'm outside.

Then you actually talk to people again 😊


[[img src="media/newspaper/articles/article-jonas-tom-offline-inseln/offline-insel" alt="Tom spends time outside without his phone."]]
---

## 🌙 Phone off after 8 pm

**Tom:** In the evenings I turn it off completely.

**Jonas:** Really completely off?

**Tom:** Yes.

Otherwise you always think of something else.

---

## 🤔 Jonas thinks

**Jonas:** I think … I'd get nervous.

**Tom:** That's habit.  
It goes away.

**Jonas:** And what does it do for you?

**Tom:** I miss less.

**Jonas:** 😂 I don't believe that.  
You miss loads.

**Tom:** Online maybe.

But not with my friends.  
Or my family.  
Or my hobbies.

---

## 💡 Maybe for you too

[[checklist title="Try one thing"]]
- Turn off push notifications 🔕
- Set fixed online times ⏰
- Build in offline time 🌳
- Phone off in the evenings 🌙
[[/checklist]]

**Jonas:** Maybe I'll try one thing.

You too?

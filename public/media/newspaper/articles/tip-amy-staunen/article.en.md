# Bad mood from your phone? Try wonder.

Sometimes the internet puts us in a bad mood.  
It can make you angry sometimes. Other times jealous. Or sad.

[[img src="media/newspaper/articles/tip-amy-staunen/staunen1" alt="Wonder"]]


Then something helps that we all know: **Wonder.**

[[callout kind="info"]]
We experience wonder when we see or experience something that is bigger than us, or something we can admire without wanting to own it.

For example:
- a wide view  
- the sky at night  
- snow falling  
- flickering fire  
- music that gives you goosebumps  
- circus performers  
- a waterfall
[[/callout]]

When you experience wonder, something special happens:  
You think less about yourself — and more about what's right in front of you.

[[callout kind="tip"]]
🌿 How it works

When a photo on the internet annoys you:
- Put your phone down.  
- Go outside or to a window.  
- Find one thing that makes you wonder. (A really simple example: test the effect of a magnet.)  
- Just look at that thing — without taking a photo of it, without commenting on it.  
[[/callout]]


Some things don't need to be documented. Experiencing them is enough.
Many people feel calmer afterwards.  
Not because everything is perfect, but because not everything revolves around likes anymore.


[[callout kind="tip"]]
Likes compare you to others.  
Wonder connects you to the world.
[[/callout]]
